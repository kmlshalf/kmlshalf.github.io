ABOUT ME (ME).

Hi, I'm Mimi! I'm here to create interactive experiences that make people smile.

I graduated in May 2020 from UC Berkeley with a degree in Cognitive Science, minor in Computer Science, and certificate in Design Innovation.

I like to explore and learn deeply about new things. Internet rabbitholes are a regular occurence for me; once I’m hooked on a subject, I start investigating every nook and cranny i can find. This has served me well in the area of design, where so many things are still undefined and so many people are still learning. I know that there’s always more than one way to approach something, and so have broadened my own perspectives on the world around me.

My expertise mainly lies in digital design (visual design, UX design, and front-end coding), but I am always open to and interested in new areas. I have a soft spot in my heart for museums and hands-on education, which I see as an intersection point for my favorite subjects: cognitive science, education, design, and technology. I hope to one day translate this love into creating interactive learning experiences for people of all ages.

I'm currently on the hunt for my next gig. If you have any openings, I'd love to chat! You can reach me at kmlshalf@gmail.com.
